🏫 About SVPCET
Sri Venkatesa Perumal College of Engineering & Technology (SVPCET), established in 2001, is promoted by the Tamilian Education Academy. Located in Puttur, Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh, it has grown into a premier integrated interdisciplinary technological institute spread across 25 acres. The campus features well-ventilated classrooms, state-of-the-art laboratories, and well-maintained outdoor and indoor sports facilities.

SVPCET is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur (JNTUA) and recognized by UGC and AICTE. It is accredited by NAAC and approved by NBA, reflecting its commitment to quality education. The college recruits only distinguished academicians, research scientists and experienced faculty — with 70% of faculty ratified by the affiliating university.

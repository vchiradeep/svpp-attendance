🏫 About SVPCET
Sri Venkatesa Perumal College of Engineering & Technology (SVPCET), established in 2001 and promoted by the Tamilian Education Academy, is a premier integrated interdisciplinary technological institute spread across 25 acres in Puttur, Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh. Affiliated to JNTUA and recognized by UGC & AICTE, the college is accredited by NAAC and approved by NBA. With 70% of faculty ratified by the affiliating university, SVPCET recruits only distinguished academicians, research scientists, and experienced faculty committed to academic excellence.

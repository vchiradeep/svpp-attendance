🏫 About SVPCET
Sri Venkatesa Perumal College of Engineering & Technology (SVPCET), established in 2001, is a premier interdisciplinary technological institute spread across 25 acres in Puttur, AP. Affiliated to JNTUA, recognized by UGC & AICTE, accredited by NAAC and approved by NBA.

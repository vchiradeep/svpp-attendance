🏫 About SVPCET
Sri Venkatesa Perumal College of Engineering & Technology (SVPCET), established in 2001 by Tamilian Education Academy, is a premier interdisciplinary technological institute spread across 25 acres in Puttur, Chittoor District, AP. Affiliated to JNTUA, Anantapur, recognised by UGC & AICTE, and accredited by NAAC (B++ Grade) and NBA. The college offers Diploma, B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA and MCA programs with 1675 total seats across 23 courses. The library houses over 10,000 books, 100+ journals, and 500+ memoirs.
